dribdat is a web app for impactful sprints and hackathons. Collect play-by-play details of collaborative events, track progress of your teams, and document the results in real time.

Follow the action on GitHub, Twitter, OpenCollective or the newsletter.

  • With all your challenges and projects on one page, you can get clean, well-referenced profiles of your work, and generate digital certificates to bring home your impact.
  • Sync your data from GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Etherpad, HackMD, DokuWiki, Google Docs & more! Easily embed screenshots, collaboration apps and social media content.
  • Automatic and manual validation tracks progress along the way, with the option of applying your own system of steps and milestones.
  • Every commit counts! Featuring a built in mechanism for sharing "Dribs" - short updates - as well as Single-Sign On and integration with GitHub, Slack and Teams.
