dribdat

The open source way to better_prototypes();

dribdat is a software package for impactful sprints and hackathons. Easily publish details of collaborative events, track progress of teams, and document the results in real time.

Follow the action on GitHub or Twitter or OpenCollective.

  • Sync your data from GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Etherpad, HackMD, DokuWiki, Google Docs & more!
  • Automatic and manual confirmation helps you to track the progress of every team along the way.
  • Incorporate your own system of data wrangling steps, agile development milestones, or other validation goals.
  • Generate clearly referenced online user profiles and distribute digital certificates to your participants.
  • Every commit counts! Featuring Single-Sign On and integration with GitHub, Slack and Teams.
It's all open

Keep your project data where-ever you want. Track progress across the world's most popular open platforms. Did we mention that it is all open source (MIT)?
